# EV Search Criteria (template)

> This is a starter template. Copy it, fill in your own needs, and the skills read
> from it (or from a project's `brief.md` created by `/ev-new-project`). Everything
> below is an **example** — replace it with your own.

## Context

Briefly describe what you're replacing and why your needs are changing (range,
space, towing, charging, etc.). Example: "Replacing a small city EV with limited
range; need more range and the ability to carry longer cargo."

Where are you based? (This tool is tuned for the **Danish market** — FDM tests,
Bilbasen pricing, DKK.) Note your home charging (e.g. 1-phase vs 3-phase / 11 kW AC).

## Budget

- **Preferred:** <min> – <max> DKK
- **Realistic max:** <max> DKK

## Hard Requirements

- Electric only (BEV)
- Minimum <NNN> km WLTP range
- <e.g. tow hitch available (factory or aftermarket)>
- <e.g. turning radius max 10.5 m>
- <e.g. heated steering wheel, auto-dimming mirrors>
- 4–5 seats
- Available for purchase in Denmark

## Preferred

- Body type preference (e.g. SUV / small crossover / hatchback)
- <e.g. can carry long items inside — fold-down seats or long cargo area>
- <e.g. matrix headlights, heated seats, roof rails>

## Brand Notes

Optional research flags — brands to give extra scrutiny (not excluded), or brands to
exclude outright. Example:

- **<Brand>**: EXCLUDED — <reason>.
- **<Brand group>**: needs extra quality/reliability scrutiny — <reason>.
- Note data-privacy stance and DK service network for brands where it matters.

## Tire Preferences

- Prefer quality all-season tires (Danish "helårsdæk") for year-round balance, or
  state your own preference (summer/winter/all-season).
- Brand preference (optional), e.g. Michelin / Goodyear tier.
- Per-car files should capture tire size and approximate pricing for a quality set.
